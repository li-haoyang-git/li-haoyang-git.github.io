Haoyang Li is currently pursuing his bachelor’s degree in Microelectronics at the Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech) in Shenzhen, China. He is a visiting student at the University of California, Berkeley from August 2024 to May 2025. His research interest is analog/mixed-signal integrated circuit design.
